# AI Assistant

Connect your own model endpoint and let AI lend a hand while you write. Your key stays local and requests go straight to the service you configured — Mox never handles your data.

## Configure

1. Open Settings › AI.
2. Enter the base URL and API key for an **OpenAI-compatible** endpoint.
3. Set the model name, temperature and other parameters. Self-hosted gateways and third-party compatible endpoints both work.

## Use

- **Inline completion (ghost text)**: once enabled, a gray suggestion appears at the caret — press **Tab** to accept, keep typing to ignore.
- **AI panel**: select a passage to summon it for continue, polish, translate, shorten/expand, explain, and more; the result can replace or be inserted.
- **Slash actions**: type **/** on an empty line and pick an AI command (e.g. Continue, Summarize) to act on the current context.

## Tips

- AI is optional — everything else works without a configured service.
- Adjust the model/temperature per task: lower temperature for steady polishing, higher for divergent brainstorming.
- The more focused your selection, the more controllable the result.
