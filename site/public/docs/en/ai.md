# AI Assistant

Connect your own model endpoint and let AI lend a hand while you write.

## Configure

1. In Settings › AI, enter the base URL and key for an OpenAI-compatible endpoint.
2. Customize the model name, temperature and other parameters; self-hosted and third-party compatible endpoints work.

## Use

- **Inline completion**: once enabled, ghost suggestions appear as you write — press Tab to accept.
- **AI panel**: select text to summon it for continue, polish, translate, summarize, and more.
- **Slash actions**: type `/` on an empty line to trigger common AI commands.

> Your key is stored locally and requests go straight to the service you configured — Mox never handles your data.
