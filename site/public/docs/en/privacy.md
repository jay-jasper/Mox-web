# Privacy Policy

Mox is a local-first macOS editor. Your documents live on your own device — Mox does not collect, upload, or sell your content.

## What we don't collect

- No document content, file names, or editing activity.
- No built-in analytics or telemetry.
- No accounts — there's nothing to sign up for or log into.

## Optional online features

A few features reach the network only after you configure and use them, and the requests go straight to the third-party service you chose:

- **AI Assistant** — when you invoke an AI feature, the relevant text is sent to the AI provider you configured (e.g. OpenAI). Whether to enable it, and which provider, is up to you.
- **Image upload** — when you upload an image, it's sent to the image host you configured.
- **Website & changelog** — opening these from the Help menu visits this site in your system browser.

These requests follow each third-party service's own privacy policy; Mox never handles or stores that data.

## Local storage

Preferences, themes, and the recent-files list are stored in your local macOS user directory and never leave your device.
