# Quick Notes

Quick Notes are floating sticky notes you summon instantly — capture an idea before it's gone.

## Summon

The default global shortcut `⌥⌘N` creates a new note (customizable in Settings).

## Manage

- **Pin to screen**: click 📌 in the note's title bar — pinned notes stay on top and won't auto-collapse.
- **Dock to edge**: drag a note to the screen edge; it collapses into a slim drawer. Click the little arrow to slide it back out.
- **Save to Desktop**: save a note as a `.md` file on your Desktop; further edits sync automatically.
- **Notes list**: open the manager from the menu bar or the note's list button to search, open, and delete.

> After you close the main window, Mox stays resident in the menu bar so Quick Notes are always within reach.
