# Resume Studio

Write a résumé the structured way: fill in the form on the left, see it typeset live on the right, export ready to send.

## How it works

1. Create a résumé and pick a template.
2. Fill sections on the left: basics, education, experience, projects, skills…
3. The right pane previews the paginated result, WYSIWYG.

## Arrange

- **Drag to reorder** sections.
- **Show/hide** sections you don't need.
- **Multiple templates** — switch and your content reflows into the new layout.

## Export

- Export to PDF (custom page and margins) or to an image.
- Single- or multi-page, with page breaks that avoid splitting entries.
