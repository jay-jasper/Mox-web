# Export

Mox exports your documents to several formats.

## Built-in export

- **PDF**: customizable page size, margins, headers and footers.
- **HTML**: styled or plain.
- **Image**: export the document or a selection as PNG.

## Pandoc formats

With Pandoc installed, export to Docx, LaTeX, EPUB, and more.

> Choose a target in File › Export. Before exporting PDF, confirm the layout in the preview.
