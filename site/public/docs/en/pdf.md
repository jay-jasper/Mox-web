# PDF Reading & Notes

Read PDFs and annotate them in the same window — leave traceable notes as you go.

## Open

- Drag a PDF into the window, or double-click a `.pdf` in the file tree.
- Single- or two-page mode; invert colors under dark themes to ease the eyes.

## Annotate

- **Highlight**: select text to highlight, in several colors.
- **Notes**: attach a text note to a selection.
- **Screenshot**: capture a region, with OCR text recognition.
- Annotations are saved with the PDF and persist next time.

## Into your notes

- Export highlights and notes to Markdown to fold them into your knowledge base.
